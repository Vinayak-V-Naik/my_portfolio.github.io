Expense Tracker System

A simple yet powerful full-stack web app that helps users record daily expenses, view analytics, and manage their budget efficiently.

Project Overview

The Expense Tracker System allows users to record, update, and analyze their spending habits. It offers category-wise and month-wise analytics with a simple, intuitive interface. The project demonstrates a complete full-stack architecture with cloud deployment.

The Problem

  • Managing daily expenses manually is time-consuming and error-prone.
  • Lack of centralized tracking makes it hard to monitor spending habits.
  • No clear visualization of how much is spent by category or month.
  • Difficult to plan budgets effectively without analytics and trends.

The Solution

  • Simple user interface: Built an easy-to-use web app where users can add, update, and view expenses by date.
  • Data management: Implemented a backend database to securely store expense details with categories, amounts, and notes.
  • Analytics by category: Enabled users to analyze their spending between selected dates and identify major spending areas.
  • Monthly insights: Added monthly trend analysis to help users understand and optimize their financial habits.
